I believe in harnessing the power of lived experiences with the practice of design to create solutions that enhance people's lives in inclusive ways, in ways that matter. I leverage my skills and experience designing in startup environments for over 4 years and my past lives in in biomedical engineering, clinical research, and education to ensure a holistic approach and final product that satisfies the business, our team(s), the target audience, and my standard of quality. Curious to know the full story of how my prior backgrounds brought me to the design industry? Read the more detailed account of the journey here on Medium.

I am inspired by more than just pixel-perfect and purposeful design. I lean on art, nature, cooking, writing, even gaming at times, to broaden my design perspective while simultaneously sharpening my eye and perception of what makes design more than just 'pretty,' but positively impactful. As such, I believe in these design philosophies when doing the work:

Maximize help, minimize harm
Team-centric >>> ego-centric
Intentionality & inclusivity
Other reasons I'd be a great addition to your team:
  • I (re)built this portfolio manually with HTML/CSS/JS. This means I (especially as a not-software-engieer) am resourceful and tenacious. I can also understand, empathize, communicate, and enjoy working with developers to ensure products are built to not just MVP, but MSO (minimum satisfactory outcome) before further iteration and refinement
  • I'm an aspiring Taekwondo Black Belt. This means I believe in constant practice (or iteration), dedication, patience, and collaboration to work and grow together
  • I'm a Liverpool FC fan. This means I strongly believe in cooperation and teamwork to reach the goals that teams and the business set, with tact, strategy, and flair (and that I'm loyal when things aren't going so great for the team)
  • I'm a collector. This means I seek creative inspiration beyond digital designs. I appreciate the craftsmanship of figures, cinematography and plot in films, compositions in music, or word choice and structure in books
  • I'm one of relatively few to have '100%ed' Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice. This means I am relentlessly determined and dedicated to seeing projects and ideas through to the best possible outcome
